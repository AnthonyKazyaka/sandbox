# Why Build a Game Backlog Tracker?

## üéÆ Personal Motivation

### The Real Problem
Like many gamers, I've accumulated a massive backlog:
- **Steam library**: 200+ games (mostly unplayed)
- **Xbox Game Pass**: 300+ games available
- **PlayStation Plus**: Dozens of monthly games
- **Nintendo Switch**: Physical and digital games gathering dust
- **Epic Games**: Free games every week adding up

**The result?** Analysis paralysis. I spend more time browsing what to play than actually playing.

### What I've Tried
- **Spreadsheets**: Too manual, not engaging
- **Existing apps**: Too complex or missing key features
- **Mental lists**: Forget what I wanted to play
- **"I'll just play [newest game]"**: Backlog keeps growing

### What I Need
A tool that:
1. **Shows me the full picture** - Visualize entire backlog
2. **Helps me decide** - Smart suggestions for what to play next
3. **Tracks my progress** - See completion rates and trends
4. **Motivates me** - Make backlog management fun
5. **Works offline** - No internet required, privacy-first
