Game of Life - Ancestry & Age Visualization

Click cells to toggle them. Colors represent ancestry (hue) and age (darkness).

How it works:

  • Ancestry: Each cell's hue represents its lineage, inherited from a random living neighbor when born
  • Age: Cells get darker as they age (older cells are darker, minimum 20% lightness)
  • Rules: Standard Conway's Game of Life (B3/S23) - birth with 3 neighbors, survive with 2-3 neighbors
  • Interaction: Click cells to toggle them alive/dead
