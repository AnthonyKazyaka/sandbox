How to Play:

  • Goal: Clear all living cells (colored squares) by hitting them with the ball
  • Controls: Use arrow keys or mouse to move the paddle
  • Evolution: The pattern evolves following Conway's Game of Life rules, but only when the ball hits your paddle
  • Scoring: Gain points for destroying cells, bonus points for clearing patterns
  • Challenge: Balance clearing cells with letting the pattern evolve strategically
